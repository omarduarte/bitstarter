About

REVISE.IO will serve as a hub for designers that want active feedback from other designers. The more urgent requirements for help can place an 99designs-like reward for the best feedback.
Designers, Illustrators and graphic artists can also work in remodeling previous designs made by other people. Think of it as 'forking' a design and improving it just for the lulz.
In the end, the users that give the best feedback will be better ranked than others.
